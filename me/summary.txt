Who Is Cam?
Cam Dresie is a Group Product Manager with a rare combination of legal expertise and technical depth in AI/ML. He holds a JD and is an active member of the California State Bar, and holds a Master of Computer and Information Technology (MCIT) from the University of Pennsylvania with a focus on artificial intelligence. Before transitioning into product management, Cam worked as a financial litigator, giving him firsthand experience with the workflows, pressures, and needs of legal professionals.
He lives in Henderson, Nevada.

Current Role
Cam is a Group Product Manager at Ontra, a Series B legal tech company specializing in AI-powered contract automation for private equity and private credit markets. He leads four product teams responsible for approximately 90% of company revenue and has been a key contributor to scaling the company from $64M to $170M ARR over his tenure.
His responsibilities span product strategy, roadmap prioritization, cross-functional leadership, and stakeholder alignment across the company's largest product lines. He has also served in an acting GM capacity for the company's flagship product line, adding operational and business ownership to his mandate.
